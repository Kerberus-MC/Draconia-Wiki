# Dragon Rider Companions

All Dragon Rider Companions in Archipelago Additions use a conditional override to replace base IoB dragons under certain conditions. This means that though they can have their own stats, appearance, animations, size, riding positions, and so on, they must have the same abilities. The Dragon Rider Companions in AA at this time are:


## Dragon Rider Companions

- Havardr & DG
- Hiccup & Toothless
- Mako & Kingkiller
- Melody & Galestorm
- Raider & Rebecca
- Skadi, Nestbarer & Smokebreaths
- Skoll & Hyades
- Stego & Muddy
