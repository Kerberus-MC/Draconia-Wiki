# Dragons

See also: Dragon Rider Companions.

All dragons in Archipelago Additions use a conditional override to replace base IoB dragons under certain conditions. This means that though they can have their own stats, appearance, animations, size, riding positions, and so on, they must have the same abilities. The dragons in AA at this time are:

Alpha and titanwing variants (including the Mountain King Goregutter have the following stat increases:

- Health: 20 points (10 hearts)
- Armour: 2 points (1 icon)
- Attack damage: 2 points (1 heart)
- Flying speed: 0.01
