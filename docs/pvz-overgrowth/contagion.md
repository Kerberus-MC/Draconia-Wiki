# Contagion

The **Contagion** mod adds a zombie infection mechanic to PvZ Overgrowth. When a zombie hits you, there is a chance you get infected — and if the timer runs out, you die instantly. This guide explains how infection works and how to cure it.

[:fontawesome-solid-link: Contagion on CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/contagion){ .md-button }

---

## Getting Infected

When a zombie hits you there is an **80% chance** you get infected. Armor lowers this chance by up to **60%** (with full Netherite). Once infected, a timer starts counting down — if it reaches zero, **you die instantly**.

### Protection HUD

A small HUD display shows your current protection value against infections. For example, a value of 20% means roughly 8 out of 10 zombie hits will infect you. The display can be enabled/disabled and repositioned in the client config settings.

## Curing the Infection

There are multiple ways to deal with the infection, from a quick fix to a full cure.

### Quick Fix: Drink Milk

On our server, **drinking milk** will immediately stop the infection. This is the easiest option if you're in a pinch and have a bucket handy.

### Step 1: Get Contagious Flesh

Kill zombies — there is a **7.5% chance** they drop **Contagious Flesh**. The Looting enchantment adds **2.5% per level**.

<div class="img-center" markdown>
<img src="/assets/images/contagion/kb-img-contagious-flesh-drop.png" width="400">
</div>

### Step 2: Smelt into Heated Zombie Flesh

Cook the Contagious Flesh in a furnace to get **Heated Zombie Flesh**, which is no longer contagious.

<div class="img-center" markdown>
<img src="/assets/images/contagion/kb-img-heated-flesh-smelting.png" width="300">
</div>

### Step 3: Craft Gold Streaked Zombie Flesh

Combine the Heated Zombie Flesh with Gold Ingots to get **Gold Streaked Zombie Flesh**. Eating it **resets the infection timer**, but does **not** cure you.

<div class="img-center" markdown>
<img src="/assets/images/contagion/kb-img-gold-streaked-crafting.png" width="300">
</div>

<div class="img-center" markdown>
<img src="/assets/images/contagion/kb-img-gold-streaked-effect.png" width="300">
</div>

### Step 4: Brew The Cure

Combine Gold Streaked Zombie Flesh with an **Awkward Potion** in a brewing stand to get **The Cure**.

<div class="img-center" markdown>
<img src="/assets/images/contagion/kb-img-cure-brewing.png" width="300">
</div>

### Step 5 (Optional): Extend the Cure

Add **Redstone** to The Cure to create **Cure Potion II**, which lasts **3x as long**.

---

## Summary

| Method | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Milk** | Stops infection immediately (server only) |
| **Gold Streaked Flesh** | Resets timer, does NOT cure |
| **The Cure** | Fully cures infection |
| **Cure Potion II** | Fully cures, lasts 3x longer |
