# Plant Spawn Mechanics

Plants spawn naturally when chunks first load based on biome conditions. Unlike mobs, they do **not respawn continuously** and generate **only once per chunk** during terrain creation. This encourages exploration across different regions.

## Key Mechanics

- Each plant type ties to specific environments
- Already-explored chunks won't generate new plants
- Rarer plants appear only in particular biomes
- Exploration is essential for progression
