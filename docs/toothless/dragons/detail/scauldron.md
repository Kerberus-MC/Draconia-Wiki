# Scauldron

<img src="/assets/images/toothless/kb-img-scauldron.png" width="120" style="float: right; margin-left: 1rem;">

The Scauldron is a main canon dragon, first appearing in the Book of Dragons short, and receiving canon confirmation by appearing in HTTYD3. It is an Archipelago Additions dragon on theSkrillbase.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Health | 145 (72.5 hearts) |
| Armour | 6 (3 icons) |
| Attack | 6 (3 hearts) |
| Walk Speed | 0.2 (Cow speed) |
| Fly Speed | 0.15 (around 50% faster than Allay speed) |

## Taming

**Taming Foods:** Blue Oleander

**Requirements:** Decrease dragon below 25% hp

**Alt Taming:** Dragon Nip

## Breeding

**Breeding Foods:** Suspicious Stew, Sagefruit

## Drops

**Brush Drops:** 2-3 Scauldron Scales, 1 Scauldron Venom

**Death Drops:** 1-3 Dragon Blood, 4-7 Scauldron Scales, 2-3 Scauldron Venom

## Spawn Biomes

- `minecraft:beach`
- `minecraft:snowy_beach`
- `minecraft:stony_shore`
