# Light Night

<img src="/assets/images/toothless/kb-img-light-night.png" width="120" style="float: right; margin-left: 1rem;">

The Light Night is an Archipelago Additions original hybrid of the Light Fury & Night Fury. It is on the Night Light base.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Health | 70 (35 hearts) |
| Armour | 0 |
| Attack | 4 (2 hearts) |
| Walk Speed | 0.4 (Piglin Brute speed) |
| Fly Speed | 0.2 (twice Allay speed) |

## Taming

**Taming Foods:** Raw Cod, Raw Salmon, Tropical Fish

**Requirements:** The dragon must be a baby

## Breeding

**Breeding Foods:** Honeycomb, Sagefruit

## Drops

**Brush Drops:** 1-3 Light Night Scales

**Death Drops:** 1-2 Dragon Blood, 3-5 Light Night Scales

## Spawn Biomes

- `This dragon does not spawn. It has a 50% chance to be produced when breeding a Light Fury and a Night Fury . The alternative outcome is a Night Light .`
