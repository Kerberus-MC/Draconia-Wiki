# Boneknapper

<img src="/assets/images/toothless/kb-img-boneknapper.png" width="120" style="float: right; margin-left: 1rem;">

The Boneknapper is a short film canon dragon, first appearing in the Legend of the Boneknapper. It is on theMonstrous Nightmarebase.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Health | 110 (55 hearts) |
| Armour | 14 (7 icon) |
| Attack | 8 (4 hearts) |
| Walk Speed | 0.44 |
| Fly Speed | 0.14 |

## Taming

**Taming Foods:** Bone

**Requirements:** No tools or weapons (except Dragon Blades & specified Viking Artifacts ) held
No armour worn
Fire resistance potion effect active

**Alt Taming:** Dragon Nip

## Breeding

**Breeding Foods:** Suspicious Stew, Sagefruit

## Drops

**Brush Drops:** 2-3 Boneknapper Scales, 2-3 Bones

**Death Drops:** 1-3 Dragon Blood, 4-7 Boneknapper Scales, 7-13 Bones

## Spawn Biomes

- `minecraft:dripstone_caves`
- `minecraft:dark_forest`
- `minecraft:swamp`
- `minecraft:stony_shore`
