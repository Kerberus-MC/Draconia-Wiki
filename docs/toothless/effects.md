## Blue Oleander

Inflicts damage over time to dragons. Acts like the Wither Effect but slower, only harms dragons, and scales in damage the longer it's active. Cure by right-clicking an afflicted dragon with **Scauldron Venom**.

**Immune dragons:** Flightmare, Shockjaw, Scauldron, Skrill, Skrillcutter, Skrillknapper

When a dragon gets the effect, they take 4 damage (2 hearts) initially. Every 10 minutes, a damage tick occurs that increases by 50% compound each time. Eventually the damage will one-shot the dragon.

## Dragon Blood Scent

Prevents a player from directly interacting with dragons for the duration (typically 30 minutes). **Cannot be removed with milk or commands** — only dying or waiting it out.

Players cannot command, ride, or tame any dragons while afflicted. Indirectly commanding dragons with Dragon Command Items bypasses this effect.

## Dragon Root

Makes affected dragons hostile towards other nearby susceptible dragons. Cure by right-clicking with a **Dragon Root Remedy**.

**Immune dragons:** Crimson Goregutter, Gronckle, Hotburple, Rumblehorn, Scuttleclaw, Thunderbottom

| Cause | Duration | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Dragon Root Crop | 00:25 | In range, difficulty above Peaceful |
| Dragon Rooted Dirt | 00:50 | Standing on the block, difficulty above Peaceful |
| Wild Dragon Root | 00:50 | Standing on the block, difficulty above Peaceful |

## Refined Dragon Root

Forces dragons to land, cripples movement, and kicks riders off. Prevents remounting until cured. Wild dragons under this effect can be fed without meeting taming requirements, but can't be ridden. Cure with **Dragon Root Remedy**.

**Immune dragons:** Crimson Goregutter, Gronckle, Hotburple, Rumblehorn, Scuttleclaw, Thunderbottom
